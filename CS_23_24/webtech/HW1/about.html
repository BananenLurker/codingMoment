About us

We are UITGEVERIJ, based in The Netherlands, forged by our founders Daan van der Putten, Simon de Jong and Yoeri Hoebe. We believe in the power that books hold to see new perspectives, learn about the world around us and offer us an escape of our everyday lives. We are lucky to have a team around us who share the same passions and hold the same believes we do. More about them can be found below! If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us!
