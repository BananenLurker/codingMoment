Our favourite Stephen King books

Carrie

Carrie White, a shy high school outcast, discovers her telekinetic powers while enduring relentless bullying. When a cruel prank pushes her over the edge, she unleashes her terrifying abilities in a vengeful rampage that culminates in a catastrophic prom night.

It

In the town of Derry, a group of childhood friends confronts an ancient evil entity that preys on their deepest fears, often taking the form of Pennywise the Dancing Clown. Reuniting as adults, they must confront their past traumas to defeat the malevolent force.

Salem's Lot

Writer Ben Mears returns to his childhood town of Salem's Lot, only to find it plagued by an epidemic of vampirism. As the undead spread their influence, Ben and a small band of survivors must fight against the darkness before it consumes them all.

The Green Mile

Set on death row at Cold Mountain Penitentiary during the 1930s, the narrative revolves around prison guard Paul Edgecombe and his encounter with John Coffey, an enigmatic inmate possessing extraordinary healing powers. Their fateful connection leads to profound moral and existential questions.

The Shining

Jack Torrance, a struggling writer, accepts a job as the winter caretaker of the remote Overlook Hotel. Isolated with his family, Jack's sanity deteriorates as malevolent forces within the hotel manipulate his vulnerabilities. His son, Danny, possesses psychic abilities that unlock the hotel's terrifying secrets, leading to a desperate battle for survival.
